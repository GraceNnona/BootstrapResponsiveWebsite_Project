About Us

Welcome to The Riverside, where the vibrant flavors of Mexico and Italy come together to create an unforgettable dining experience. Nestled along the river’s edge, our restaurant celebrates the art of blending two rich culinary traditions—offering a fusion of bold, fresh Mexican ingredients with the comforting, time-honored flavors of Italian cuisine, and a hint of Asian inspiration to round out our diverse menu. At The Riverside, we pride ourselves on using only the finest locally-sourced ingredients to craft dishes that honor the best of all worlds. Start your meal with our refreshing Asian Shrimp Salad, tossed in a zesty ginger sesame dressing, or enjoy a hearty Vegetable Stir-Fry served with rice and a comforting bowl of soup—perfect for a light but flavorful start. For those who crave tradition, we offer a classic Spaghetti Bolognese, slow-cooked to perfection with a rich, savory sauce that brings the taste of Italy right to your table. Our cozy, riverside atmosphere combines the rustic charm of Italy with the vibrant energy of Mexico, offering a relaxed yet elevated space where friends and family can come together to enjoy great food, excellent wine, and unforgettable moments. Whether you're joining us for a casual dinner by the river or celebrating a special occasion, The Riverside is your destination for a one-of-a-kind culinary adventure. Join us, and experience the perfect harmony of flavors from three distinct cultures—right at the water’s edge. Buon Appetito, ¡Buen Provecho, and Enjoy!
